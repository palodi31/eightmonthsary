Love Letter

To my babi,

Eight months with you already feels like a lifetime of warmth wrapped into the gentlest kind of love. You came into my life and suddenly even ordinary days started feeling softer, brighter, and worth looking forward to. The smallest things became special because they had you in them — your voice, your laugh, your little updates, your presence, your love.

Thank you for being the kind of person who makes my heart feel at home. Thank you for the comfort you give without even trying, for the sweetness you carry so naturally, and for the way you make love feel safe, exciting, and real all at once. I love the way you stay in my thoughts long after our conversations end, the way your smile can fix the heaviest parts of my day, and the way loving you feels like choosing the most beautiful thing over and over again.

If I could, I would bottle every memory we have made so far — every moment that made me grin like an idiot, every quiet second that made me realize just how lucky I am to have you. These eight months are not just a number to me. They are proof that what I feel for you keeps growing, deepening, and becoming even more certain.

I hope you always remember this: you are loved so deeply, so sincerely, and so intentionally. I admire you, I cherish you, and I will keep finding new ways to make you feel how important you are to me. No matter how busy life gets or how ordinary a day may seem, my heart will always make room for you first.

Happy 8th monthsary, babi. Thank you for being my favorite person, my soft place, and one of the best parts of my life. I love you more than these words can fully hold — and I will keep loving you in all the quiet, loud, simple, and magical ways I know.

Forever yours,
Your Love
